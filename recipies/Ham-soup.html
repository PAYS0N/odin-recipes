Ham Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups diced ham
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 onions
  • 3/4 cup diced carrots
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 32 oz pinto beans
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups Spinach
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, basil, parsley, mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground sage, pepper

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients but spinach to the crockpot
  2. Cook on low for 6 hours
  3. Add spinach for 5 minutes
  4. Serve
