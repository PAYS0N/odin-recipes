Ham Soup

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Beans
  • Chicken Broth
  • Seasoning
  • Wine

Instructions

  1. Fry the ham
  2. Deglaze pan with wine
  3. Add the butter, garlic, onions, carrots, celery, seasonings, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce and cook for 5-6 minutes
  4. Add the beans and chicken broth
  5. Bring to a boil
  6. Add ham
  7. Simmer for 30 minutes
  8. Serve
