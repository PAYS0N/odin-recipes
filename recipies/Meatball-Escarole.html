Meatball Escarole Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 lb meatballs
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 5 cups chopped escarole
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 cup orzo

Instructions

  1. Saute onions for 5 minutes
  2. Add carrots, celery, and garlic, cooking for 5 minutes
  3. Stir in tomato paste and escarole until escarole is wilted
  4. Pour in chicken broth and bring to boil
  5. Add meatballs and simmer for 30 minutes
  6. Stir in Orzo and cook until pasta is tender
  7. Serve
