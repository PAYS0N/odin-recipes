Meatball Escarole Soup

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
  • Chicken Broth
  • Escarole
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Orzo

Instructions

  1. Finely slice the escarole
  2. Finely chop carrot
  3. Heat broth until boiling
  4. Stir in escarole, orzo, carrot, and meatballs and return to boil
  5. Reduce heat to medium and cook at slow boil, stirring frequently to prevent sticking, until pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes
  6. Serve
