Peanut Butter Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb green beans
  • 1 lb chicken
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 cup canned coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 2 lb Spagetti

Instructions

  1. Begin boiling water
  2. Slice the green beans in half
  3. Toss chicken with curry powder
  4. Add spagetti to water, reduce heat and cook until tender
  5. Stir fry chicken for 2 minutes
  6. Add beans and 1/2 a cup of water, bring to boil and simmer for 5 minutes
  7. Whisk together the coconut milk, the peanut butter, and the soy sauce.
  8. Add to chicken and beans and simmer for 5 minutes
  9. Pour chicken mixture over spagetti
  10. Serve
